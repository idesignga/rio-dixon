Who is Rio Dixon?

The Man With a Plan

DeMario Dixon is a Dekalb county alumni, Morehouse grad and the Founder of Beyond Performance Academy, a Certified Psychology Leadership Coach, and a seasoned Mindset Coach with over 8 years of experience working with student-athletes and teams. As a dynamic speaker and published author, he is passionate about helping young leaders strengthen their identity, mindset, and emotional resilience. Before launching his coaching career, DeMario played his collegiate ball at Morehouse College, and professional football with the Winnipeg Blue Bombers, private workout with the Green Bay Packers, and Montreal Alouettes, where he developed the mental tools that now fuel his mission to empower others on a field, classroom, or a stage.
